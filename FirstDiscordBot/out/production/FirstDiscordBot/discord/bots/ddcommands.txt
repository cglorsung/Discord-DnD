Prefix commands with two asterisks and dd [ex. **ddhelp]
ddhelp - get this message
ddprocess - explains how to enter the dungeon properly
rollDice:x - roll a die
ddRollTraits - roll a d20 for character traits
ddMyStats - get your stats
ddMyInv - get your inventory
ddEquipWeapon - equip a weapon from your inventory
ddEquipArmor - equip an armor from your inventory
ddweapons - print all of the weapons
ddGetNWeapon - get a random normal weapon from the list
ddGetAdvWeapon - get a random advanced weapon from the list
ddarmors - get the list of armors
ddGetArmor - get a random piece of armor
ddGetPlayers - print all of the current players
ddNewGame - reset all variables
ddSaveGame - save all variables (will overwrite any previous save file)
ddLoadGame - load last save
